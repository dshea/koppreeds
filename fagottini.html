Fagottini and Tenoroons

FAGOTTINI AND TENOROONS
Donna Agrell, Thomas Drescher and Martin Kirnbauer
Schwabe Verlag Basel, Schwabe Verlagsgruppe AG, Basel, Schweiz, 2025

Click here to order.

More than 130 small-sized bassoons from the 18th and 19th centuries are documented in this book of illustrated essays. The hardbound book includes ten pages of glossy color plates in addition to many other illustrations.

The articles are the outcome of several research projects conducted at the Schola Cantorum Basiliensis between 2017 and 2023. Two of them ("Fagottini and Tenoroons - Small Forgotten Giants" and "Out of the Bass Register - Uncovering the Organology, Pedagogy, and Performance Practice of Small-Sized Bassoons from the 18th and 19th Centuries") were supported by the Swiss National Science Foundation; a third and smaller one ("Neue alte Klangkörper") was funded by private foundations.

The authors include members of the SCB research team (Donna Agrell, Thomas Drescher, Áurea Domínguez, Giovanni Battista Graziadio, Zoë Matthews-Visentin, Letizia Viola) and experts on various aspects of the topic (David Gasche, Klaus Hubmann, James Kopp, Vincenzo Onida, Ricardo Simian).
